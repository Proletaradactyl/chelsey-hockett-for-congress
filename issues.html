Her Platform

Fighting for What Matters.

Chelsey isn't in this for headlines or political games. She's running because working families in North Texas deserve real representation on the issues that affect their daily lives.

Economic Justice

The tax system is rigged. Corporations and billionaires exploit loopholes while working families in North Texas pay more than their fair share. Chelsey believes that if you work hard and play by the rules, the government shouldn't be taking more from you than from a hedge fund manager.

"No working family should pay a higher tax rate than a billionaire. That's not America — that's a rigged game."

Chelsey will fight to:

  • Close corporate tax loopholes that let big companies pay nothing while small businesses pay full rate
  • Raise taxes on incomes over $400,000 and restore the top marginal rates for the ultra-wealthy
  • Eliminate special capital gains rates that benefit Wall Street investors over wage workers
  • End offshore tax shelters that let corporations dodge billions in U.S. taxes every year
  • Expand the Earned Income Tax Credit for working families and individuals

The federal minimum wage hasn't kept up with the cost of living for decades. A full-time worker making minimum wage today cannot afford rent, groceries, or childcare in most of North Texas. That's not a personal failure — it's a policy failure.

Chelsey supports raising the federal minimum wage to $25 per hour, phased in responsibly so small businesses can adjust — with stronger support for small business owners who are themselves struggling to compete with corporate giants.

  • Raise the federal minimum wage to $25/hour with a phased timeline
  • Index the minimum wage to inflation so it never falls behind again
  • End the subminimum tipped wage that leaves service workers vulnerable
  • Strengthen overtime protections so employers can't skirt fair pay rules
  • Support small business owners with tax credits during the transition period
Health & Security

As a mother, Chelsey knows the fear of a sick child and an unaffordable copay. No family should ever have to choose between their health and their rent. The United States is the only wealthy nation where medical bills are a leading cause of personal bankruptcy — and that's a choice our government has made. Chelsey will fight to unmake it.

"Health care is a human right. No parent should lie awake at night afraid to take their child to the doctor."

  • Pass Medicare-for-All to guarantee comprehensive coverage for every American
  • Cap the cost of prescription drugs — no more $300 insulin for a drug that costs $10 to make
  • Protect and expand reproductive healthcare access for all women
  • Expand mental health coverage and end the stigma of seeking help
  • Negotiate drug prices directly with pharmaceutical companies

Social Security is a promise this country made to its workers — and Chelsey will fight to keep it. Politicians in Washington have been threatening to cut or privatize Social Security for years. Chelsey believes we should be expanding it, not dismantling it.

  • Expand Social Security benefits — especially for low-income seniors and widows
  • Lift the payroll tax cap so the wealthy contribute their fair share to the fund
  • Oppose any efforts to privatize or cut Social Security or Medicare benefits
  • Strengthen disability protections so no American falls through the cracks
  • Increase survivor benefits so families aren't left behind
Education & Families

Chelsey is a mom first. She knows that when schools are underfunded, it's her kids — and yours — who pay the price. She opposes voucher programs that drain money from public schools to fund private ones, and she will fight for full federal investment in every public school in District 5.

"My kids go to public school. Your kids go to public school. Let's fund them like they matter — because they do."

  • Fully fund Title I schools in low-income communities across North Texas
  • Oppose school voucher schemes that strip funding from public schools
  • Fight for competitive teacher pay so we stop losing great educators
  • Expand pre-K and early childhood programs for working families
  • Cancel student debt and make community college tuition-free
  • Invest in vocational and trade education as a real career pathway

Working parents in North Texas are being squeezed from every direction. Childcare costs more than rent. Paid leave is a perk, not a right. And too many parents — especially moms — are forced to choose between their career and their kids. Chelsey refuses to accept that as normal.

  • Pass universal paid family and medical leave for all workers
  • Make childcare affordable — cap costs at 7% of family income
  • Expand the Child Tax Credit to put money back in parents' pockets
  • Protect workers' rights to organize and bargain collectively
  • Support affordable housing investment in District 5 communities
Environment & Community

North Texas families depend on clean water, clean air, and healthy land. Corporate polluters have spent decades lobbying to weaken environmental protections — and the communities that pay the price are working-class neighborhoods, not wealthy ones. Chelsey will fight to hold polluters accountable and protect the natural resources that belong to all of us.

  • Enforce and strengthen the Clean Air Act and Clean Water Act
  • Hold corporate polluters accountable with real consequences — not just fines they can afford
  • Invest in clean energy jobs that pay well and stay in North Texas
  • Protect public lands and waterways from industrial exploitation
  • Prioritize environmental justice in communities most affected by pollution
  • Support farmers and rural landowners with conservation programs
