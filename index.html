About Chelsey

Chelsey Hockett is a mom who lives in East Texas who knows firsthand what it means to stretch a budget, navigate a broken healthcare system, and worry every day about the quality of education her kids are receiving as our public schools get defunded by voucher scams.

She's not a career politician. She's one of us and is running for Congress because working families deserve someone in Washington who's actually living their reality.
