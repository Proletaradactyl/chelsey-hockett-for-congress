About Chelsey

I'm a mom who lives in East Texas who knows firsthand what it means to stretch a budget, navigate a broken healthcare system, and worry every day about the quality of education her kids are receiving as our public schools get defunded by voucher scams.

I'm not a career politician. I'm running for Congress because working families deserve someone in Washington who's actually living their reality.
