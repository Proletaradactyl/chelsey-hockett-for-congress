About Chelsey

A Mom on a Mission.

Chelsey Hockett is a mother of two from North Texas who knows firsthand what it means to stretch a budget, navigate a broken healthcare system, and worry about the schools her kids attend every day.

She's not a career politician. She's one of us who is running for Congress because working families deserve someone in Washington who's actually living their reality.

Fighting for What Matters

Fair Taxation

End the rigged tax system that rewards billionaires while working families carry the burden. Chelsey will fight to make the wealthy pay their fair share.

Protect Our Resources

Clean air, clean water, and protected land for future generations. North Texas families deserve a government that safeguards what we have.

Learn More →

Fully Fund Public Schools

Every child deserves a great public education, not vouchers that drain our schools. Chelsey will fight for full funding and well-paid teachers.

Medicare-for-All

Healthcare is a human right. No family should choose between a doctor visit and next month's rent. Chelsey will fight for universal coverage.

$25 Minimum Wage
